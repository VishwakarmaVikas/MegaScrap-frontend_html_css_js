WHAT SCRAP WE DEAL IN

PAPER

Derived from the pulping of wood or fibrous materials, paper scrap encompasses a wide range of waste products including newspapers, books, cartons, and magazines.

Plastic

Comprising a diverse range of synthetic polymers, plastic scrap is categorized by its resin type, such as HDPE, LDPE, PVC, and PET, each with distinct properties and applications.

Matel

A valuable resource rich in industrial metals, scrap metal primarily consists of ferrous and non-ferrous alloys, with steel, copper, aluminum, and zinc being the most common components.

E-Waste

Characterized as discarded electronic equipment, e-waste comprises a complex mixture of valuable recoverable materials and hazardous substances, including televisions, computers, and mobile phones.
